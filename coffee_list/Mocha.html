Mocha

Description

Espresso with chocolate and steamed milk.

Ingredients

  • Espresso – A single or double shot as the base.
  • Steamed milk – Similar to a latte, but slightly less milk than usual.
  • Chocolate syrup or cocoa powder – Mixed with the espresso to create a chocolatey flavor.
  • Whipped cream (optional) – Often added on top for extra creaminess and sweetness.
  • Chocolate shavings or drizzle (optional) – For garnish and presentation.
