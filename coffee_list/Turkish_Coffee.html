Turkish Coffee

Description

Strong, rich, unfiltered, with a smooth finish.

Ingredients

  • Finely Ground Coffee - Typically 1-2 heaping teaspoons per cup.
  • Water - Cold water, about 3 ounces per cup.
  • Sugar (Optional) - To taste, often added before brewing (none, medium, or sweet).
  • Cardamom (Optional) - Sometimes added for extra flavor.
